É necessário ter o EcmaScript 6 ou superior para ser possível ultilizar palavras reservadas do node.js
EcmaScript 6 or higher is required to be able to use nodejs reserved words

step by step:
1 - download jshint extension in vsCode
2 - In visual studio Code:
    click in 'File' > click in 'Preferences' > click in 'Settings' > click in icon 'Open Settings UI' at the top right (hold the mouse over to view the file name)
3 - configure json in settings.json:
    "jshint.options": {
        "esversion": 6
    },
4 - save
